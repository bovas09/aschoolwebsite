Josephites Of Woods

St Joseph's EMHSS Thrikkakara had its inception in the year 1963 by the Carmelite sisters of St.Teresa(CSST), a community started by Servant of God Mother Teresa of St.Rose of Lima. The school is one of the oldest in the region and it continues to be one of the premium educational institutions in the district. The schools strive to impact quality education without distinction of religion caste or creed. The school grew in leaps and bounds, ensuring high levels of academic achievement for the students. Josephites of woods with its Motto" For God and country " will certainly go out as responsible citizens with human values. The institution continues to prosper in every way through the intercession of its patron St. Joseph and the blessings from the Almighty.





Facilities




Digital Class

It is the era of smart classrooms which make learning fun. This mode of learning and teaching is most suitable for students who wish to go the extra mile to grasp information, that is a way ahead of their course curricula. Online resources can improve the curiosity and creativity of students.

Solar Panel

We have installed solar power panels to ‘Go Green’ and to save the natural resources. Our school mostly depends on solar panels for our electricity needs.


Play Ground

We have a large and landscaped playground that provides our students with a healthy means of dealing with emotion and stimulates children to use their creative energy to interact with one another.

Music & Dance Room

Musical training plays an important role in a child’s overall development intellectually, socially, and emotionally. Students are encouraged to develop their talents and skills from an early stage.
